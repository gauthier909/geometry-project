Isoperimetric theorem

The isoperimetric theorem says that of all convex n-gons of a given perimeter, the one which maximizes area is the regular n-gon. (source : https://mathworld.wolfram.com/IsoperimetricTheorem.html)

If we accept all n \(\in \mathbb{N}\), we have that the convex n-gon that maximize the area is the infinite-gon which is the circle.
